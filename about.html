About Calculator Euphoria

A small, fast toolbox — no clutter.

Calculator Euphoria is a collection of free, focused calculators for the math you actually run into: figuring out a mortgage payment, splitting a bill, checking a BMI, working out a GPA, or converting units on the fly.

What we're not

We're not trying to be a giant portal with hundreds of near-duplicate tools, autoplay video, or pop-ups between you and your answer. Every calculator on this site is built by hand, runs entirely in your browser, and shows its work so you can sanity-check the result.

How the calculators work

Everything here runs client-side in plain JavaScript. Nothing you type — income, weight, grades, loan amounts — is transmitted to a server, logged, or stored. Close the tab and it's gone.

Accuracy

We aim for correct, standard formulas (the same ones used in finance and health textbooks), and we link to the formula on every calculator page. That said, results are estimates for informational purposes — for anything with real financial, medical, or legal consequences, confirm with a qualified professional. See our Terms of Use for the full disclaimer.

Feedback

Spot a bug or want a calculator we don't have yet? This is a one-person project and still growing — suggestions genuinely shape what gets built next.
