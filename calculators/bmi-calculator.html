How BMI is calculated

Metric: BMI = weight (kg) ÷ height (m)²
Imperial: BMI = 703 × weight (lb) ÷ height (in)²

BMI categories (adults)

CategoryBMI
UnderweightBelow 18.5
Normal weight18.5 – 24.9
Overweight25 – 29.9
Obese30 and above

BMI is a quick screening tool, not a diagnosis. It doesn't distinguish muscle from fat, so it can overestimate body fat in very muscular people and underestimate it in older adults who've lost muscle mass. Talk to a doctor for a fuller picture of your health.
